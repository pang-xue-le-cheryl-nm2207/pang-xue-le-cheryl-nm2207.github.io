HOW PEOPLE WOULD LIKE
TO MEET THEIR POTENTIAL PARTNERS?

When asked about their preference of how they want to meet their potentially partner, singles clearly favoured in-person, face-to-face interactions as the ideal starting point. Meeting via social / recreation activities, getting introduced by a friend or colleague were the top three most prefered ways. Connecting through online means ranked lower than the offline means.
